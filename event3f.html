Cancer Talks

Health and Nutrition
Hosted by
Tanya Sharma April 27,2020

Feelings of sadness, anger, confusion, and helplessness come with a cancer diagnosis. For the person who has been diagnosed with cancer, it is helpful when friends and family members provide a comforting presence and practical support. It is often difficult for others to know what to say or start a conversation with someone who has cancer. However, staying in touch is always better than staying away.

Cancer Talks are arranged for educating people about the effects of cancer and to provide them an insight in the lives of people suffering from cancer. Various causes and cancer related therapies are discussed in order to make create awareness about cancer in the society. We are organising Cancer Talks 2020 , come join us and lets bring a change in the society.
